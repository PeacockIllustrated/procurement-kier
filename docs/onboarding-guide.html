Logging In

Access the portal from any device with a web browser.

  1. 1Open your browser and navigate to the portal URL provided by your team.
  2. 2Enter the password provided by your administrator.
  3. 3You’ll land on the Construction Signage homepage showing all available categories.
Tip: The portal works on phones and tablets too — handy for ordering on-site.

Browsing Products

Find the right sign by category or search across 800+ products.

  1. 1Select a category from the homepage to see all products in that group.
  2. 2Each product shows an image, code, name, and starting price.
  3. 3Tap any product to see all available sizes and materials.
  4. 4Use the search bar in the header to find products by name or code.
Tip: Product codes (like PCF151) match the codes in your existing signage catalogues.

Selecting & Adding Products

Choose your size, material, and quantity, then add to your basket.

  1. 1On the product page, find the variant you need (size and material are listed for each).
  2. 2If the product has custom fields (e.g. Site Name), fill them in — they’re required before adding.
  3. 3Set your quantity using the + and − buttons.
  4. 4Tap Add to Basket. A confirmation toast will appear.
  5. 5The basket icon in the header updates with your item count and running total.
Tip: Some products like site entrance boards require you to enter your site name — this gets printed on the sign.

Checkout

Tell us who’s ordering and where it’s going.

  1. 1Tap Proceed to Checkout from your basket.
  2. 2Under Contact Details, select an existing contact from the dropdown — or tap Add new to create one.
  3. 3Under Site Details, select the delivery site — or tap Add new to enter the site name and address.
  4. 4To edit or remove saved records, select Manage contacts… (or Manage sites…) from the dropdown.
Tip: Contacts and sites are saved for future orders. Set them up once and reuse them every time.

Review & Submit

Add purchaser details, review your order, and submit.

  1. 1Under Purchaser, select the person responsible for raising the purchase order — or tap Add new.
  2. 2Optionally enter a PO Number if you have one.
  3. 3Add any Special Instructions (delivery notes, timing, etc.).
  4. 4Review the Order Summary — it shows every item, subtotal, VAT (20%), and total.
  5. 5Tap Submit Order. You’ll see a confirmation screen with your order reference number.
Tip: Your order reference (e.g. PER-20260318-A1B2) is your tracking number — save it or find it later on the Orders page.

Requesting a Custom Sign

Need something bespoke? Describe it and we’ll quote you.

  1. 1Tap Custom Sign in the header navigation.
  2. 2Select the Sign Type (Warning, Prohibition, Mandatory, etc.) and Shape (Rectangle, Square, Circle, or Triangle).
  3. 3Enter the Sign Text — this is the message that will appear on the sign.
  4. 4Choose your Size and Material, then set the quantity.
  5. 5The Preview panel updates in real time as you make changes.
  6. 6Tap Add to Basket — Quote on Request. The sign is added at no charge; our team will confirm pricing before manufacture.

Tracking Your Orders

Check the status of any order without picking up the phone.

  1. 1Tap Orders in the header to open the My Orders page.
  2. 2Filter by contact (tap a name pill at the top) or site (tap a site card).
  3. 3Use the status tabs to filter: All, New, Awaiting PO, In Progress, Completed.
  4. 4Tap any order card to expand it and see full details: items, pricing, contact info, and current status.

Uploading a Purchase Order

When a purchase order is needed, you’ll receive an email with an upload link.

  1. 1You’ll receive an email titled “PO Request” with the order details and a link.
  2. 2Click the Attach Purchase Order button in the email.
  3. 3You’ll be taken to the upload page. Select your PO document (PDF or image).
  4. 4Click Upload. The document is attached to the order and the team is notified.
Tip: The upload link is unique to this order. No login required — just click and upload.

Managing Contacts & Sites

Keep your contact and site records up to date for fast repeat ordering.

  1. 1During checkout, select Manage contacts… (or Manage sites… / Manage purchasers…) from the relevant dropdown.
  2. 2A modal opens showing all saved records.
  3. 3Tap the edit icon to update a name, email, phone number, or address.
  4. 4Tap the delete icon to remove a record. Past orders are not affected.
  5. 5To add a new record, tap Add new and fill in the details.
Tip: Any changes you make are available immediately for all future orders.
